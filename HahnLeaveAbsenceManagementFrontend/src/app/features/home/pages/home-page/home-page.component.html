FAQs

How do I cancel a request?

Use the app’s request page or contact HR with your request ID.

Can I carry over days?

Up to 5 days can be carried over to the next year, subject to approval.

What documents are required?

Sick leave over 3 days needs a medical note; other types vary by policy.

How far in advance should I apply?

Submit at least 3 business days before the start date; 10+ days for extended leave.

What if my leave overlaps a public holiday?

Public holidays within approved dates don’t count against your leave balance.

Can I edit or withdraw a submitted request?

You can edit or withdraw while it’s Pending; after approval, contact HR.
